# Project - Cozy Coop Bullet Hell

## Outcome

A game (single player, multiplayer via steam) with bullet hell mechanics, loot drops, seeded random terrain and enemy spawns, dungeons tied to consumable keys, consumable stat increase potions. Includes soft-core mode, and various QoL improvements suitable for small scale coop and “cozy” game vibes while paying respect to the original gameplay loop.

## Why it matters

- I personally find the game to be fun but too disrespectful regarding player time investment

- This is a great next game development project for me in terms of scope, marketability, and personal development

- There is a significant unsatisfied demand in this category in particular, and I think the gaming market will be receptive to a well crafted product in this category
