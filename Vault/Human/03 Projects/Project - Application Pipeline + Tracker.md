# Application Pipeline

## Outcome

I have the tools to effectively apply to varied job postings and track my output independently for follow-up and analysis. If you have gaps, they will be identified so you can address them through skill building, portfolio projects, or creative (but always accurate) language in hopes of the personal sell in interviews.

## Why it matters

In today’s job market, you need to put out numbers, and hiring processes are more automated than ever. Being able to navigate the behind-the-curtains view of the job seeking process, and incorporate knowledge about things like ATS keywords and industry expectations, allows me to represent myself in the most favorable light possible.

	- Claude has a routine to create a tailored resume and cover letter for a job listing
