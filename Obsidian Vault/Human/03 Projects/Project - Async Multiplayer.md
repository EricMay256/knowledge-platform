# Async Multiplayer Framework

## Outcome

The project can support async multiplayer games with adapters for multiple consumers including Unity and Unreal. This involves play-by-mail, player keeps with sieges, selecting a matchmade opponent, and a multi-stage autobattler setup.

## Why it matters

Many types of multiplayer don’t depend on live connections, and these allow for interesting interactions and provide organic competition and player choice/feedback. Having the bones created enable a project that would use it to lean heavily upon it, and even have its specs guided by this interface
